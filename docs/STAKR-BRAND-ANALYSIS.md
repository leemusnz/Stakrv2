## 📊 **Accessibility Check**

### **Contrast Ratios (WCAG 2.1):**

| Combination | Ratio | WCAG AA | WCAG AAA |
|-------------|-------|---------|----------|
| Orange on White | 3.5:1 | ⚠️ Large text only | ❌ Fail |
| Cyan on White | 3.2:1 | ⚠️ Large text only | ❌ Fail |
| Orange on Black | 6.8:1 | ✅ Pass | ⚠️ Large text only |
| Cyan on Black | 4.9:1 | ✅ Pass | ❌ Fail |
| Slate on White | 12.5:1 | ✅ Pass | ✅ Pass |

**Recommendation:** 
- ✅ Use orange/cyan for buttons, badges, highlights
- ✅ Use slate (#334155) for body text
- ✅ Never use orange/cyan for small text on white
