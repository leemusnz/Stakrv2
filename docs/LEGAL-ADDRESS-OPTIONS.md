### **Option 3: PO Box** 📬
**Cost:** $20-80/year (USPS)  
**Setup Time:** 30 minutes  
**Good For:** Budget option

**How to get:**
- Go to local post office
- Fill out USPS Form 1093
- Pay annual fee
- Get PO Box number

**What you get:**
```
Stakr Inc.
PO Box 12345
Your City, State 12345
```

**Pros:**
- ✅ Very cheap ($20-80/year)
- ✅ No home exposure
- ✅ Official USPS address
- ✅ Easy to set up

**Cons:**
- ⚠️ Less professional than street address
- ⚠️ Can't use for LLC in some states
- ⚠️ Some people won't send to PO Box

---

### **Option 4: Registered Agent** 🏛️
**Cost:** $100-300/year  
**Setup Time:** 1-2 days  
**Good For:** LLC/Corporation requirements

**Providers:**
- **Northwest Registered Agent** - $125/yr
- **Incfile** - $119/yr
- **ZenBusiness** - $99/yr + $199/yr address
- **Harbor Compliance** - $299/yr

**What you get:**
- Registered agent for LLC (legal requirement)
- Business address for official documents
- Accept service of process
- Forward legal mail

**Use for:**
```
Stakr Inc.
c/o Northwest Registered Agent
123 Legal Street
Dover, DE 19901

Contact: legal@stakr.app
```

**Pros:**
- ✅ Required if you have LLC anyway
- ✅ Professional
- ✅ Handles legal documents
- ✅ Privacy protected

**Cons:**
- 💰 Annual cost
- 📋 Usually tied to LLC formation
