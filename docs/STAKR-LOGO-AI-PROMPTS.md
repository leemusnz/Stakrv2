## 🏆 **PRIMARY PROMPT: "The Stack" Logo**

### **Full Logo (Icon + Wordmark)**

```
Create a modern, bold logo for "STAKR" - a challenge-based accountability app where users stake money on achieving personal goals.

ICON DESIGN:
- Geometric ascending triangles stacked vertically, forming a pyramid/mountain shape
- 3 layers of triangles, getting larger from top to bottom
- Top small triangle: #FFD700 (victory gold) - represents achievement
- Middle triangles: #00BCD4 (bright cyan) - represents progress  
- Bottom/base triangles: #FF5722 (vibrant orange) - represents stakes/foundation
- Sharp, precise geometric shapes with clean edges
- Negative space between layers for modern look
- Icon should work at 16px (favicon) and scale up beautifully

WORDMARK:
- Text "STAKR" in bold, geometric sans-serif font
- All caps, tight letter spacing
- Strong, confident presence
- Color: #334155 (dark slate) on light backgrounds
- Positioned to the right of icon or below

STYLE:
- Minimalist, modern, tech-forward
- Flat design (no gradients, shadows, or 3D effects)
- Vector-ready, clean lines
- Think: Stripe, Notion, Linear logos (that aesthetic)
- Professional but energetic

SYMBOLISM:
- Triangles pointing up = growth, ambition, achievement
- Stacked layers = building habits, progress over time
- Solid base = strong foundation, stakes
- Ascending size = momentum, leveling up

OUTPUT:
- White or transparent background
- SVG-ready vector style
- Should look great in orange monochrome OR full color
- Scalable from 16px to billboard size

AVOID:
- Realistic textures or 3D effects
- Gradients (flat colors only)
- Curved or organic shapes (keep geometric)
- Cluttered or complex details
- Sports cliches (no dumbbells, trophies, or medals)
```
